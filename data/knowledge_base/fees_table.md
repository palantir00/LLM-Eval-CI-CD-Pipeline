# Fees

## Account maintenance
The basic account plan is usually free, with no monthly maintenance fee. Higher plans, which
offer additional benefits, are paid monthly according to the current price list.

## International transfer fees
The fee for an international transfer is not a single fixed amount. It depends on the
currency, the amount, the destination country, and the customer's chosen pricing plan. It is
always charged according to the current price list.

## ATM withdrawals
ATM cash withdrawals are typically free up to a certain monthly limit. Above that limit, a
fee may apply according to the price list and the customer's plan.
