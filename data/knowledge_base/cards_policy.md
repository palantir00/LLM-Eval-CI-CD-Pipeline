# Cards Policy

## Virtual cards
A virtual card exists only in the app and is used mainly for online payments. It is
available immediately after issuance, so the customer can start paying online right away
without waiting for anything to be delivered.

## Physical cards
A physical card is a plastic card sent to the customer by post. It can be used to pay in
brick-and-mortar shops and to withdraw cash from ATMs, in addition to online payments.

## Blocking a card
A lost or stolen card can be blocked instantly by the customer in the app, in the card
management section. The block takes effect immediately. After blocking, the customer can
order a new card if needed.

## Card spending limits
Customers can set and adjust their own card spending limits in the app, for example daily
payment limits or limits for online and ATM transactions, to increase control and security.
