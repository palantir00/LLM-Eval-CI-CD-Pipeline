# Account Opening

## Required documents
To open an account, the customer usually needs a valid identity document, such as a national
ID card or a passport, and a selfie used for identity verification. The entire onboarding
process takes place in the app.

## KYC verification
KYC (Know Your Customer) is the process of confirming a customer's identity. It is required
by anti-money-laundering (AML) regulations. Before fully granting access to its services, the
bank must verify the customer's identity document and personal data.
