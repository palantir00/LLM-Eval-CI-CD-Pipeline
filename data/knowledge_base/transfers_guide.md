# Transfers Guide

## Domestic transfers
A standard domestic transfer in the local currency is usually settled within a few hours on
business days. Instant transfers, sent via the instant payment system, arrive within
seconds, including outside the bank's working hours and on weekends.

## SEPA transfers
A SEPA transfer covers euro payments within the SEPA area (the single euro payments area in
Europe). It is usually fast and cheap, and is the standard way to send euro within Europe.

## SWIFT transfers
A SWIFT transfer is used for international payments in various currencies outside the SEPA
area. It can be slower and more expensive than a SEPA transfer because intermediary
(correspondent) banks may charge additional fees along the way.
